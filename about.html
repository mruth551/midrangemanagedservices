About Midrange Managed Services

Midrange Managed Services specializes in consulting and managed services for organizations running IBM i environments on IBM Power Systems. Our mission is to help businesses maintain secure, reliable, and high-performing systems that support mission-critical operations.

With extensive experience administering IBM i systems across multiple industries, we help organizations improve system stability, security, and performance while reducing operational risk.

Our Expertise

  • IBM i system administration
  • Performance optimization
  • Security hardening
  • Backup and disaster recovery planning
  • PTF and OS lifecycle management
